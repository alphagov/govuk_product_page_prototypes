Available registers

You can use any of the following registers to build your service.

Country register

A list of British-English names and descriptive terms for countries.

Local authority England register

A list of local authorities in England.

Local authority type register

A list of types of local government organisations in the United Kingdom.

These registers are in beta. Other registers (including registers of charities, schools and territories) are currently in discovery and alpha.
