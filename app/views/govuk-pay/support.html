GOV.UK Pay support

If your problem is not addressed in the technical documentation, please contact us at govuk-pay-support@digital.cabinet-office.gov.uk, including any error messages you’re getting.

Starting a trial account

If you want to start using GOV.UK Pay send us an email with the following information:

  • your name, email address and phone number
  • your government organisation name
  • the service(s) you want to use GOV.UK Pay with

Newsletter and blogs

GOV.UK Pay publishes a newsletter every few weeks. This will help you stay updated with any new features we release.

We’ll also blog about our progress using the Government as a Platform blog or the GDS blog.
