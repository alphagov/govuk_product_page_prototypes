Take and process payments

GOV.UK Pay is simple to integrate, reliable and secure. It’s built to meet the Digital Service Standard, designed for user needs, and GOV.UK-branded.

See how to get started

-

You can benefit from GOV.UK Pay if you want to:

  • take online card payments and make refunds
  • ensure your payment experience meets the Digital Service Standard
  • get advice on switching payment providers
  • build a new service with an end-to-end GOV.UK experience
  • use payment processes that are fully PCI DSS compliant
  • get expert advice from Government Digital Service

See GOV.UK Pay’s top features and roadmap

Teams building services will get:

  • clear documentation on how to integrate with GOV.UK Pay
  • a sandbox (test) environment to prototype and try with your service
  • 24/7 support from the technical team at Government Digital Service

See how GOV.UK Pay works in our documentation

Teams running services and managing payments will get:

  • an admin dashboard for reporting and refunds
  • GOV.UK- branded payment pages for their users
  • guidance on using GOV.UK Pay to process payments efficiently
