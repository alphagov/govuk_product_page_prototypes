Designed for real people

GOV.UK has users at its heart and is a trusted name. Our accessible GOV.UK payment pages are built to the Digital Service Standard, and tested for ease of use, and reliability.

Works seamlessly with your service

The GOV.UK Pay service component uses a RESTful API and simple documentation for easy technical integration. Which means it’s easy to start taking payments and integrate workflows with other case management systems.

Who’s using GOV.UK Pay

Central government organisations currently using GOV.UK Pay are:

  • Ministry of Justice
  • Office of the Public Guardian
  • Companies House
  • Home Office

GOV.UK Pay is not yet available to local authorities.
