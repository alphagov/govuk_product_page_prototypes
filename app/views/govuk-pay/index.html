Take and process payments for your service

GOV.UK Pay helps the public and businesses to pay for government services in a consistent way. It also makes it quicker and easier for service teams to process these payments and manage queries.

Try it today

Benefits of GOV.UK Pay

Service teams using the GOV.UK Pay admin site can:

  • email receipts to users
  • easily handle payment queries
  • select different debit and credit card types
  • issue refunds
  • reconcile payment records
  • generate financial reports for a range of dates

Users paying for a government service will get:

  • a more consistent payment experience across government
  • a choice of how to pay
  • refunds more quickly
  • payment pages designed to be accessible and easy to use

Who's using GOV.UK Pay

These central government organisations are currently using GOV.UK Pay:

  • Companies House
  • Home Office
  • Ministry of Justice
  • Office of the Public Guardian

GOV.UK Pay is not yet available to local authorities.

How it works

GOV.UK Pay integrate with the different payment service providers so your team won’t have to. This makes it simpler for your team to take online payments and reduce processing costs.

Integrating GOV.UK Pay with your service

GOV.UK Pay provides technical documentation to help your service:

  • use the API
  • integrate your service with GOV.UK Pay
  • run tests with the code
  • check code structures

Try it out

Ask to create an account and test the service. You’ll need to be part of a central government organisation to do this.

With an account you can:

  • test out the admin site
  • look at public-facing payment pages
  • add team members and give them different permissions

Contact the team
