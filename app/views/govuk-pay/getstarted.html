Get started

    1. Check GOV.UK Pay is right for you

    Review GOV.UK Pay’s top features and roadmap and decide if it meets your service’s needs.

    If you are a central government organisation with a new or existing digital service and want to take card payments online then GOV.UK Pay should be suitable for you.

    2. Create an account

    Test GOV.UK Pay for yourself by creating an account.

    You can explore the test environment, and use the same account details when you’re ready to go live.

    3. Share with your team

    Share information about GOV.UK Pay with your broader team. Managing payments usually involves people from a number of different teams: digital, operational delivery and finance, for example.

    You can invite team members from your GOV.UK Pay Dashboard, and you may want to send our documentation to your technical and finance teams.

    4. Prototype and test

    Prototype and test the integration of your service with GOV.UK Pay. You’ll find clear instructions in our documentation.

    5. Contract with a Payment Service Provider

    You will need to have a contract for Payment Gateway and Merchant Acquiring services with a Payment Service Provider, who will process the payments taken using GOV.UK Pay.

    If you don’t have one already, then avoid any procurement work by signing up with the Government Banking Service. They have an existing contract that’s open to central government and health sector organisations.

    Contact us if you’d like more advice on contracting.

    Sign up with the Government Banking Service

    You’ll need to email helpdesk.gbs@hmrc.gsi.gov.uk attaching completed versions of the following forms:

    • Merchant Acquiring - New Application
    • Worldpay Gateway Setup Form
    • Worldpay Direct Debit form

    6. Configure your Payment Service Provider and GOV.UK Pay

    You will need to make sure that your underlying Payment Service Provider is set up to work with GOV.UK Pay. We have instructions for configuring your WorldPay account in our documentation. You’ll also need to choose your settings in the GOV.UK Pay admin tool.

    7. Agreements and go live

    Contact us to switch to a live account and start taking payments. Your organisation needs to sign our Memorandum of Understanding and your team needs to agree to our Terms of Use before you can go live.
