Roadmap

Here’s a list of the new functionality we’re planning to offer through GOV.UK Pay over the next 6 to 9 months.

This roadmap is a only a guide and may change from month to month.

June 2017

  • Self service account creation and team management
  • Support for Barclays online card payments (ePDQ)
  • Disaster recovery within 1 hour with an additional Amazon Web Services region

July to September 2017

  • Improved refund-related reporting
  • Reporting for settlement of funds to merchant (business) bank account
  • Basic aggregate reporting dashboards for payments
  • Support for Maestro and Visa Electron

September 2017 onwards

  • Include payment statuses
  • Foreign currency support
  • Foreign language support
  • Delayed capture (more efficient security process)
  • Refund confirmation emails
  • Recurring card payments

Queries and suggestions

If you want to find out more about these features, or have some needs that GOV.UK Pay isn’t yet meeting, please get in touch with us by emailing govuk-pay-support@digital.cabinet-office.gov.uk or chat with us in our cross-government Slack channel.
